Icon for Hide

Since the workflow hides all your Mac windows, I thought it would be a great idea to combine a down arrow with the UI elements of Mac windows.
The result? A snice shiny beautiful icon.

Tools: Cinema 4D, Photoshop, Illustrator, AppleScript

After installing Alfred 2, you can activate the workflow by typing “hide” and when you hit enter on the keyboard, watch as your windows magically disappear. Download the Hide Workflow here for free. If you like my custom theme, you can download it here.

Before this was an Alfred 2 workflow, it was a stand alone Mac app. Being that required a Mac installer, this is my design for the installer. The colors and theme matched my website at the time.
