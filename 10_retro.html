Retro Video

I made this video for Greyscale Gorilla’s 5 second animation project. Every month or so Nick Campbell chooses a theme and animators from around the world submit their video for the competition. The winner gets recognition on the Greyscale Gorilla website. Although I didn’t win, I ended up having this nice video for you to enjoy.

I had about 16 hours to do this project. In that time, I needed to think of the idea, draw the storyboard, create the assets and make the video.

Tools: Cinema 4D, Illustrator, After Effects

The animation starts with the name of the video“Retro”. Next the cylinder flies across the screen and blocks fly into view and the camera cuts to the next shot. At a close-up camera angle, we see the blocks and cylinder transform into something. The camera cuts again and with a old school hip hop kick sound, the final logo is revealed. Retro.

Here’s a glimpse at some of the tools I used to make this video. I used Cinema 4D for the 3d, animation and render. I used After Effects for the compositing, editing and effects.
