The Search Video

This animation demonstrates my understanding of animating a quadraped (4 legged character). I researched many quadrapeds and studied their movements extensively. After gather reference and knowledge, I used a free rig to do this animation.

I used Maya to build the environment, texture the scene, texture the character, light the scene, animate the character, composite the render layers, edit the sound and edit the video.

Character Rig: Javier Solsona, Tim Matias
Tools: Maya, Photoshop, After Effects, Mental Ray

The story is about a dog who follows his nose. The video begins with him following a scent. The camera pans over to reveal a bone tied to a rope. The dog stops because the scent got really strong, he looks up and sees the bone. Then crouches down and after preparing himself with a shake, he jumps after the bone. I focused on conveying character while animating the dog to move anatomically correct.
