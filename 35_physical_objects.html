Physical Objects Study

I spent 60 minutes physically studying objects around the studio. I used this exercise to practice noticing the forms and shapes. I sketched the contours with a pen to prevent erasing and redrawing. This forced me to really pay attention to the details.

Tools: Blue Bic Round Pen, Black Bic Round Pen

I used a pen for these sketches to avoid what I call eraser syndrome, constant erasing because youâ€™re unhappy with what you see. Using a pen forces me to just keep going.

I collected random objects around the office and spent about 15 minutes holding and sketching each item. I focused on the lines that make each object. By holding each item I was able to get a good sense of the contours that make each shape. I found it challenging to ignore the fine details and just sketch the outlines. Not being able to erase lines made me really think before drawing each line.
