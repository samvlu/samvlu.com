HP & Intel

This project was for the client HP. The goal was to make a 2 short video showcasing the new HP Computer. The first video was about the new Intel chip. The second video needed to demostrate how the new all in one computer can simplify a desk.

Tools: Cinema 4D, After Effects

Simple shapes with
good lighting can be cool.

I started the project by gathering reference images of the intel chip. Since the client provided images for the HP computer, my only concern was getting the chip to look like an intel chip. I used a combination of simple shapes, good lighting, and correct texturing to build an intel-like chip.

This opening scene illustrates the typical cubical desktop. Next everything on the desk fades away and the new HP computer is revealed. We notice a simpler and cleaner desktop layout. I put together this scene with objects from Idea Integrationâ€™s 3d models library. I arranged the objects in Cinema 4D and rendered the scene with simple ambient occlusion. The objects are all animated with After Effects.
