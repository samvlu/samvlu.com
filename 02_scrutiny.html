The Scrutiny Video

This is a short animation with focus on lip syncing which is matching character animation to a sound clip. This is normally the process in 3d animated films. An actor or actress records the voice acting and animators use the voice to guide the character animation.

I used Maya to build the environment, texture the scene, texture the character, light the scene, animate the character, composite the render layers, edit the sound and edit the video. Special thanks to my friend Israel Woolfolk who helped with lighting and critique.

Character Rig: Rodri Torres, C.J. Cow
Additional Lighting: Israel Woolfolk
Tools: Maya, Photoshop, After Effects, Mental Ray

This story begins right after a kid failed his tests and got a lecture from his father. The dad asks “Is that clear?” and the kid nervously replies with babbles. The father explodes in anger and demands “Stop babbling boy!” I focused on getting the characters to show their emotions through their facial expressions, body language and movement.
