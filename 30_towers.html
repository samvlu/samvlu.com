Alex Volkov Towers

I was inspired by the work of a fantastic designer, Alex Volkov and decided to recreate his towers in 3d. It proved to fun and a great learning experience.

Tools: Cinema 4D, Photoshop, Illustrator

I started by studying Alex Volkovâ€™s Towers by sketching it on paper. By doing so, I learned the proportions of the buildings and the size of the elements. Then I used Illustrator to construct the curves of of each building from the top view. Next I opened Cinema 4D and used the curves to build the foundation of each building. Most of the work was done in Cinema 4D. This includes UV mapping, materials, modeling, lighting and rendering. Finally I used Photoshop for the compositing.

One of the biggest challenges was getting the windows to line up correctly because the towers have an organic curved shape. The wireframe here shows the uneven distances between each polygon face. Adding the windows with a texture proved to be more work that just putting on a window texture.
