Nibs Video

This video was for the client Moli. The goal was to create the pilot episode of what would be a series of webisodes. I worked on this with my friend Justin Falgout. My contribution was modeling, character rigging, animation, compositing and video editing. The client provided the concept, storyboard, character designs, audio and creative direction.

Tools: Maya, Zbrush, After Effects, Photoshop

The most challenging part of this animation was building the rigs. Each character needs their own unique facial expressions. To ensure that this is possible, I spent a lot of time setting up controls that would allow for specific expressions.
