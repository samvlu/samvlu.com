Sofa Concepts

I started this project to explore 2 techniques. The first technique is building objects with simple shapes. And the second technique is setting up lighting that affects mood. My approach for each scene went in this order: 3d modeling, create unique materials and light the scene.

Tools: Cinema 4D, Photoshop

changing the lighting, changes everything...

By focusing the lights from above, we end up with beautiful highlights along the top rim of the sofa and the cushions. Since the lights are targeted to a specific area, the focus is on the sofa and not the environment. The result, a grandiose mysterious feel.

This sofa has lighting from the above, slightly in front and behind. The result is nice highlights, semi-lit front and softly lit environment. This still has a mysterious feel but we now get a sense of the room.

By having lights from above, we get nice highlights on this sofa as well, but its less grandiose because the entire room is lit evenly.
