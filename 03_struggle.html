The Struggle Video

This a short animation about the struggle of our main character Sergio. The goal was to animate a bi-ped while focusing on showing weight and personality. I modeled the environment, textured the scene, textured the character, lit the scene, animated the character, composited and edited the video. Special thanks to my friend Israel Woolfolk who helped with lighting and critique.

Character Rig: Tim Linklater, Joel Smith, Lisa Griffiths, Andy Seredy
Additional Lighting: Israel Woolfolk
Tools: Maya, Photoshop, After Effects, Mental Ray

The story starts with Sergio standing under a pull-up bar with the spotlight on him. Prior to this he had just bragged to friends that he could easily do 10 pull-ups. He jumps up and grabs the bar, but to his surprise he can barely pull himself up. Through a brief struggle, he manages to do 1 pull up and drops to the ground. He feels really embarrassed.
