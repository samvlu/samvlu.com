Thinking Of You

Feeling inspired by Valentine’s Day, I wanted to make an expressive art piece for someone special. The idea was to illustrate the road to love in a symobolic way.

Tools: Cinema 4D, Photoshop

The road to love is illustrated as a golden road with no guard rails, it’s inviting yet risky. If you are able to survive the windy road and reach the top, you’ll find 2 huge hearts holding each other and sharing 1 light. Both hearts reflect the light back at each other, creating highlights that make them both look even better.

Once I had the idea I immediately sketched many concepts until I found the right one. Next I built the main components (the road and hearts) in 3d. I made the environment in Photoshop. The made the textures and materials with Cinema 4D and Photoshop. This image illustrates the 3d parts in Cinema 4D.
