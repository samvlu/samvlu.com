Design with Letters

For these projects I combined letters with 3d. I used Illustrator to create the letter paths. Cinema 4d for the 3d, texturing, and lighting. And finally I used After Effects and Photoshop to tweak colors and add additional effects.

Tools: Cinema 4D, After Effects, Photoshop, Illustrator

This is an experiment with typography, 3d and effects. I learned that simple objects can look interesting if you get the lighting right. I spent lots of time strategically placing lights to create highlights in specific places. Every rim light is created on purpose. To make this art piece even more interesting I an atmosphere and subtle depth of field.

To study how materials and light play together, I first needed a 3d object. I chose “SLUG” (abbreviation for Sam Lu Graphics) because I wanted something short and simple. I learned that materials can greatly affects the way and object looks in light. Knowing how materials absorb light can help me achieve specific effects.

This is a wallpaper I made for Idea Integration after their rebrand to “.idea” I took the new logo eps file and used it to build a 3d typographical version. I used the company style guide to decide what colors to use. I kept the backdrop clean and grey to match the aesthetics of the “.idea” brand. All these elements were created in Cinema 4D.
