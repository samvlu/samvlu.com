About

Sam is a Motion Designer at Astadia.
He’s works with clients such as Microsoft, HP and Behr.
He is also an alumni of Full Sail University.

Sam has an avid interest in web development and currently has 72 badges at Codecademy. He is self-taught and learned through countless hours of tinkering and reading. He aspires to combine his knowledge of design with web development. Languages he is well versed in: HTML, CSS, SASS, Javascript, jQuery and Ruby.
