HP ENVY 100 AiO

Art Director: Shalin Pei
Writer: Matt Walsh
Project Manager: Heather Williamson
Motion Designer: Sam Lu

This project was for the client HP and produced at Idea Integration. The objective was to make a video promoting the new HP All-in-one printer. The video was used for presentations and an introduction to a interactive product tour. My contribution was creation of 3d models, animation, motion design and video editing. I had a great time working with an awesome team.

Tools: Cinema 4d, Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects

This is one of my favorite renders from the HP video. At a glance we get a sense of the materials used to construct this printer. I did my best to match the materials to the actual printer.

This printer does more than your average printer. It comes equipped with a touch screen, scanner and it can wirelessly print from mobile devices. This printer does all that while maintaining a simple sleek look. This video highlights these features.

These 4 frames were in our original storyboard. The first frame tells a brief history of printing. Then we call out some of the features through texts that appear on the screen and animation. Finally we end the video by showing how the printer can blend in to the environment of your home.

Here's a behind the scenes glance at how this video was made. I used Cinema 4D to build and animate the 3d assets. To create the materials, I used a combination of Cinema 4D and Photoshop. And I used After Effects to handle animation of text, compositing, effects and video editing.
