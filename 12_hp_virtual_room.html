HP Virtual Room

This was a client project for HP. The goal was to create a graphic for HP’s Virtual Room software. The program lets people from offices around the world collaborate face to face through a computer.

I designed 3 different buildings to illustrate businesses from around the world. If you look closely, you’ll notice each building has trees from different parts of the world. They offices sit on top of floating platforms that are up in the clouds away from the distractions of the world. The platforms are connected by wires to represent the world wide web that connects the computers together. The environment has a painterly feel to simulate a “virtual room”.

Tools: Cinema 4D, Photoshop

I used Cinema 4D to build the 3 office buildings, platforms, wires, and trees. All textures, lighting and rendering are also done in Cinema 4D. I painted the environment using photoshop.
