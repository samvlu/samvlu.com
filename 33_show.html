Icon for Show

I created this icon for an Alfred 2 workflow that hides all your Mac windows. The idea for this icon came from combine the features of a hidden window (grayed out window UI elements) and an up arrow.

Tools: Cinema 4D, Photoshop, Illustrator, AppleScript

After installing Alfred 2, you can activate the workflow by typing “show” and when you hit enter on the keyboard, watch as your hidden windows magically reappear. Download the Show Workflow for free. If you like my custom theme, you can download it here.

Before this was an Alfred 2 workflow, it was a stand alone Mac app. Being that required a Mac installer, this is my design for the installer. The colors and theme matched my website at the time.
